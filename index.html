Remembering Steve

Over a million people from all over the world have shared their memories, thoughts, and feelings about Steve. One thing they all have in common — from personal friends to colleagues to owners of Apple products — is how they’ve been touched by his passion and creativity. You can view some of these messages below.
And share your own at rememberingsteve@apple.com

Goodbye Steve, and thanks.

Many want to change the world, and we all do to varying extents. Some for the better, some for the worse, some significantly and dramatically, others less so. Steve Jobs changed the world signifuicantly, dramatically and in many ways for the better. Because of their focus on “Computers for the rest of us” he and Steve Wozniak could have called the company they built “Prometheus Computers.” The Apple II, Lisa, Mac, Newton, iPod, iTunes store, iPod Touch, iPhone, iPad, are, in a sense, like the fire ‘stolen’ in the myth by Prometheus and given to man. Jobs, however, while known for being tough, was not known for being pompous. He will be missed by his family and friends. His ideas will be missed by the rest of us. Goodbye Steve, and thanks.

Larry from New Jersey
